# Getting Started with VWS
 
Welcome to VWS, your all-in-one solution for building and managing websites with ease. This guide will walk you through the steps to get started quickly and efficiently.

Go to browser and paste the following link to getting started with VWS: 

```{3} copy
http://localhost:3000
```

## Sign Up

To begin using VWS, you need to sign up for an account. Follow these simple steps:

Visit the VWS Website:

Open your web browser and navigate to vws.

On the homepage, click on the "Login" button located in the top right corner.

Using google account Login/Sign-Up with respective google account. 

If you are an Agency owner Then you will have to fill up Agency creation form.

Else you will be redirected directly to the VWS dashboard.

Complete your profile by providing additional information such as your name and contact details also connect with your stripe account.

## Navigation
VWS features an intuitive user interface designed for easy navigation. 
Here's a quick overview of the main sections for the Agency Admin/ Agency Owner:

Dashboard: Access your account overview and business key metrics.

Team: Manage your agency teams and invite to the user to join the team.

Settings: Configure your account preferences and notifications.

subaccounts: Manage all the clients using particular subaccounts.

Billing: Get all the details about the Subscriptions.

Here's a quick overview of the main sections for the Agency Admin/ Agency Owner:

Dashboard: Access your account overview and business key metrics.

Contacts: Manage client contacts and ticket value allocated to them.

Settings: Configure your account preferences and notifications.

Pipeline: Make pipelines for the software development lifecycle.

Funnels: Build website using custom components.

Now that you're familiar with the basics, feel free to explore VWS and start building your websites!
